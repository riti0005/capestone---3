Charmander

Body Shape:

Charmander has a compact, reptilian body shape. Start with a basic oval or egg shape for the body.

Head:

Charmander's head is slightly larger compared to its body. It has a rounded snout and a small mouth with sharp teeth. Eyes are large and expressive, usually black with white pupils. On top of its head, there's a small horn-like structure.

Limbs:

Charmander has four limbs, two arms, and two legs. The arms are short with three fingers on each hand. Legs are also short with three toes on each foot. Each limb ends in small, sharp claws.

Tail:

The most distinctive feature of Charmander is its flame-tipped tail. The tail is long and thin, with a flame at the tip. The flame can be depicted as a simple shape, like a teardrop or flame outline.

Details:

Charmander has several small details on its body, such as belly scales and ridges on its back.
